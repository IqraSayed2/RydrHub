Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our car rental services. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

You need a valid driver's license held for at least 1 year, a credit card in your name, and a form of identification (e.g., passport or national ID). International customers may need an International Driving Permit.

The minimum age to rent a car is typically 21 years old. Drivers under 25 may incur a young driver surcharge, which varies by location and vehicle type.

Basic third-party liability insurance is usually included. However, we recommend reviewing our optional coverage plans for full protection against damage, theft, and personal accident.

Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation online through our 'Manage Booking' section or by contacting our customer support team. Cancellation policies and fees may apply depending on the time of cancellation.

Yes, we offer convenient delivery and pickup services at select locations for an additional fee. Please check availability and charges when making your reservation.

Returning the car late may result in additional charges, often equivalent to an extra day's rental. We recommend contacting us immediately if you anticipate a late return.

Most of our rentals include unlimited mileage. However, some special vehicle categories or long-term rentals may have mileage caps. Please refer to your rental agreement for specific details.

We operate on a "full to full" fuel policy. This means you receive the car with a full tank and are expected to return it with a full tank. Otherwise, a refueling service charge will apply.

Yes, additional drivers can be added to the rental agreement at the time of pickup, provided they meet the minimum age and document requirements. An additional driver fee may apply.

In case of a breakdown or any mechanical issue, please contact our 24/7 roadside assistance hotline immediately. The number is provided in your rental agreement and on our website.
